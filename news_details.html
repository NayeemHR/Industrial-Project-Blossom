Blossom Food and Beverage brings you the rich fortified formula

The fight to minimize malnutrition In Bangladesh has been a generational challenge. In addition to being an official supplier of High Energy Biscuits (HEB) to the World Food Programme (WFP), we have diversified our fortified food portfolio to include fortified rice or ‘Pushti Chaal.’We have partnered with local and international organizations to play a significant part in impacting the lives of over 2 million people in 96 Upazilas as a part of the Vulnerable Group Development (VGD) project. The VGD project which is part of the government’s safety net program provides about 30 KG of rice for the families of extreme poor women and other at-risk groups.In addition, we have also invested in facilities and a state-of-the-art research lab to further the cause and to help make our agri technology and innovations affordable to the masses

Community Development

In addition to our central cause of improving nutrition in Bangladesh, we are also playing a vital role in uplifting the communities in which we operate. The founder has built various educational institutions, orphanages, and mosques in Narayanganj, Manikganj, Narayanganj etc and is involved in various charitable activities through the Mohammad Ali Chowdhury and Mostafa Ali Chowdhury Trust.We believe that sustainability is the key to long term growth and profitability
