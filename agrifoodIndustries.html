Purpose

Incorporated in 2015, Blossom Agri Food Industries have been producing high energy VITA rice infused with all the nutritional and micronutrients required for a healthy diet.

A consistent supply of fortified rice is a key milestone in our fight against malnutrition, and the vital step towards a positive social footprint.

Fortified rice is a staple ‘super food’ and is designed to form the core of a person’s diet, ensuring that the rice alone delivers all required nutritional supplements for healthy growth in children and adults.
