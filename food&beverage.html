About Food and Beverage

Blossom Food and Beverage brings you the rich fortified formula which takes care of your daily nutritional

Blossom Food and Beverage brings you the rich fortified formula which takes care of your daily nutritional needs, especially for growing children. Our products are high in dietary values and absolutely delicious! Boosting your immunity and satisfying your tastebuds.

Partners.

It falls within our core values to invest in technology and people, the pillars of our success till date. Our machinery comes imported from industry leaders in Switzerland, Spain, Italy and Japan.

Purpose

With the goal to eliminate malnutrition, we understand proper nutrition is vital to a child’s physical and mental development till the age of seven. Our products are aimed to be both yummy and healthy for your growing child, making it easier for parents to ensure their children get the nutrition they need to be healthy every single day.

Being an official supplier to the WFP and the Government of Bangladesh, you can rest assured that we maintain the highest quality standards for ingredients and the strictest food safety standards.

Product

Our portfolio of healthy snacks are forged using World Food Programme (WFP) approved recipes

Our portfolio of healthy snacks are forged using World Food Programme (WFP) approved recipes that adds essential micronutrients such as Calcium, Magnesium, Iron, Iodine, Folic Acid, Vitamin A, Vitamin B1, B2, B6, B12b to the diet. Which are commonly not consumed, but essential for a healthy brain and body development in toddler and adolescent children.
