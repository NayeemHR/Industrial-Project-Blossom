Certifications & Memberships

Certifications and International Memberships

ICA: International Cotton Association

The world's leading cotton trade association promoting safe trading and protecting the global cotton trade.

GOTS: Global Organic Textile Standard

Production certification that limits the use of toxic bleaches, dyes and other chemical inputs during the production process and ensures ethical and sustainable sourcing of organic cotton.

BCI: Better Cotton Initiative

The largest cotton sustainability programme in the world, exists to make global cotton production better for the people who produce it, better for the environment it grows in and better for the sector’s future

OCS: Organic Content Standard (OCS)

Standard that verifies the presence and amount of organic material in a final product and tracks the flow of a raw material from its source to the final product. 

OEKO-TEX 

Textile and leather test for harmful substances at all processing levels. Certifying the environmentally and socially responsible production conditions of production facilities.
